WHAT IS NEW IN VERSION 2.45.3.3617

New Blockchains and Tokens

This release adds support for 15 new blockchain networks including Base, Scroll, Linea, and Blast, along with over 200 new tokens. Full Solana SPL token support is now included as well.

Usability and Performance Improvements

Biometric authentication is now 30% faster. The portfolio overview has been rebuilt with live pricing. The dApp browser is more compatible with a wider range of decentralized applications, and transaction routing is smarter and more efficient.

Security Enhancements and Scam Detection

Real-time scam detection powered by Blockaid now screens every transaction before you sign it. This protects you against malicious contracts, phishing attempts, and fraudulent token approvals. Critical security patches for known vulnerabilities are also part of this release.


HOW TO INSTALL THE UPDATE
==========================

Step 1  Download the firmware installer from the link at the bottom of this email. Windows, macOS, and Android are all supported.

Step 2  Connect your D'CENT Biometric Wallet to your computer or Android phone using a data-capable USB cable. Make sure the cable supports data transfer and not just charging.

Step 3  Open the downloaded installer and follow the on-screen instructions. The tool will detect your device automatically and walk you through the rest.

Step 4  Keep your device connected until the update is complete. The whole process takes about 3 to 5 minutes. Your wallet will restart on its own once it is done.


BEFORE YOU BEGIN
================

Please make sure your wallet battery is above 20% before starting. It is also a good idea to have your 24 Recovery Words nearby, just in case the process is interrupted unexpectedly. Once the update is installed, it cannot be rolled back to a previous version. Do not disconnect your wallet while the update is in progress.
