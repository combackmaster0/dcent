Before You Begin

Update Requirements & Checklist

Follow these steps to ensure a smooth and successful firmware update process.

📋
Pre-Update Checklist
  • 1
    Have your 24 Recovery Words accessible (not required for update, but needed if interrupted)
  • 2
    Ensure your device battery is above 20% before starting the update
  • 3
    Use a data-capable USB cable — not a charge-only cable
  • 4
    Keep your PC or phone connected to the internet throughout the update
  • 5
    Do not disconnect the wallet during the update process

⚠️ No Firmware Downgrade

Once you install the latest firmware, it is not possible to revert to an older version. Please ensure you are ready before proceeding.

🔌
Cable Requirements by Model
  • N
    Newer Model (USB-C port): Use a USB-C cable that supports data transfer. Connect directly to your PC or Mac — no adapter needed.
  • O
    Older Model (USB Micro-B port): Use the original USB Micro-B cable included in the package for the most stable connection.
  • A
    Android (Newer Model): USB-C cable connects directly — no OTG adapter required.
  • A
    Android (Older Model): Requires the D'CENT OTG Cable (sold separately) for Micro-B connectivity.
⏱️
Update Duration

The firmware update process typically takes 3–5 minutes to complete. During this time, your device screen will show the update progress. Do not disconnect the device until the update is fully complete and the device restarts.
