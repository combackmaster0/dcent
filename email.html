How to Install the Update

1

Download the Firmware Installer

Click the download button below to get the official D'CENT Firmware Installer for your platform. Windows, macOS, and Android are all supported.

2

Connect Your Wallet via USB

Use a data-capable USB cable to connect your D'CENT Biometric Wallet to your computer or Android phone. Make sure the cable supports data transfer, not just charging.

3

Run the Installer and Follow the Prompts

Open the downloaded installer and follow the on-screen instructions. The tool will automatically detect your device and begin the update process.

4

Wait for Completion

The update takes approximately 3 to 5 minutes. Keep your device connected throughout. Your wallet will restart automatically once the update is complete.

Important: Before starting the update, please make sure your device battery is above 20% and that you have your 24 Recovery Words accessible in case the process is interrupted unexpectedly. Once installed, firmware cannot be downgraded to a previous version. Do not disconnect your wallet during the update.
